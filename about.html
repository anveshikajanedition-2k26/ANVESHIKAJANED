About Anveshika

Welcome to the January edition of Anveshika: Beyond the Boundaries — a magazine dedicated to technology, innovation, and exploration in the field of data science and beyond. Each month, we bring together insights from industry professionals, academic leaders, and students to create a platform that informs, inspires, and connects.
