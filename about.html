About Anveshika

Anveshika: Beyond the Boundaries is a knowledge-driven magazine dedicated to technology, innovation, and exploration in the evolving world of data science and artificial intelligence. Each edition brings together perspectives from industry professionals, academic leaders, and students, creating a platform that informs, inspires, and connects a growing community of learners and innovators.
