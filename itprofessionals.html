IT PROFESSIONAL • RAJ VERMA

Raj Verma: From Real-Time Data to Real-World Impact

Raj Verma is the CEO of SingleStore, a real-time data technology company enabling fast transaction, analytics, and search in a single platform. With decades of leadership experience across global enterprise tech firms, Verma has played a key role in scaling high-growth data and AI-driven organizations.

Enterprise Leadership: Held senior executive roles at Staffware, TIBCO (CMO, COO, EVP Global Sales), Apttus Software, and Hortonworks; helped grow TIBCO’s revenue beyond $1 billion. SingleStore Transformation: Appointed CEO in 2019; led the rebranding from MemSQL to SingleStore and scaled annual recurring revenue from $10M to over $100M by 2023.

AI & Real-Time Data Vision: Positioned SingleStore as a key platform for generative AI through real-time data access, vector search, and Snowflake integration. Recognition & Media: Named one of San Francisco’s Top 50 Business Leaders (2023); featured in Fast Company, WEF, Bloomberg, CNBC, Yahoo Finance, and more.

IT PROFESSIONAL • GEETHA MANJUNATH

Geetha Manjunath: Redefining Healthcare Through Data Science

Geetha Manjunath is an Indian computer scientist and entrepreneur, best known as the Founder and CEO of NIRAMAI Health Analytix. She pioneered AI-based, non-invasive and radiation-free breast cancer screening using thermal imaging. Her work combines advanced research with social impact in healthcare innovation.

Founder & CEO: Leads NIRAMAI Health Analytix, an AI health-tech startup focused on early breast cancer detection using Thermalytix Research Career: Contributed to India’s first commercial supercomputer at C-DAC; spent 17 years at HP Labs; served as Lab Director at Xerox India.

Healthcare Innovation: Developed non-touch, non-invasive screening effective for women under 45; raised ~$7M in funding from global investors. AI for Public Health: Expanded AI use to detect Covid-19, respiratory diseases, and river blindness; collaborated on X-RaySetu via WhatsApp for rural diagnostics.

IT PROFESSIONAL • ARAVIND SRINIVAS

Arvind Srinivas: Where AI Meets Knowledge

Arvind Srinivas is an Indian-American computer scientist and entrepreneur, best known as the co-founder and CEO of Perplexity, an AI-powered search technology company. He is recognized for redefining search by delivering direct, intelligent answers using advanced AI models. His work places him among the most influential young leaders in artificial intelligence today.

Research Experience: Worked on cutting-edge AI and machine learning research at OpenAI, Google Brain, and DeepMind during his academic years. Entrepreneurship: Co-founded Perplexity in 2022 to build an AI-driven, answer-focused search engine as an alternative to traditional search platforms

Leadership & Growth: Attracted major investments from Jeff Bezos, Nvidia, and other global leaders; expanding AI-powered search into smartphones through telecom partnerships. Global Recognition: Named among TIME magazine’s 100 most influential people in AI (2024). Creative & Public Impact: Collaborating with A. R. Rahman on the project Secret Mountain; announced plans to apply for U.S. citizenship in 2024.
