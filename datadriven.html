The New Literacy: Making Sense of Data in a Digital World

Learning data science has become highly accessible through online platforms offering free resources, affordable certifications, and hands-on projects. From Python and SQL to AI and machine learning, learners can build industry-ready skills at their own pace. Global platforms like Coursera, edX, Kaggle, and Infosys Springboard empower beginners and professionals alike to grow in the data-driven world.

ICDATA’25: Where the Global DATA Science Community Converges

The 21st International Conference on Data Science (ICDATA’25), part of CSCE’25, will run July 21–24, 2025 as a hybrid global event. It will host over 1,000 participants from 75 countries, featuring keynotes, tutorials, and technical sessions. Attendees gain full access to all CSCE’25 conferences, fostering collaboration between academia and industry with indexed proceedings.

- Scale & Prestige: - Recognized among the top five global gatherings in computing and applied technologies - Expected 1,000–1,500 participants from ~75 countries

- Program Features: - Keynote speeches by world-renowned scientists - Technical & research presentations - Special sessions and free tutorials - 112 sessions across 21 joint conferences

DATA-DRIVEN WORLD • DATA SCIENCE SUMMIT

Data Science Summit 2025: Shaping the Future of AI, Trust, and Governance

The Data Science Summit 2025 (DSS 2025), held on 5 December 2025 by ISB Institute of Data Science with HSBC, gathered leaders from industry, government, and academia to discuss AI, cybersecurity, and digital trust. Recognized as a Pre-Event to the India AI Impact Summit 2026, it highlighted responsible innovation and India’s vision for technology-driven governance. Keynotes showcased initiatives like the Telangana Data Exchange, AI Mission, and Quantum Roadmap, underscoring data-driven governance for sustainable growth.

- Organizers: ISB Institute of Data Science (IIDS) + HSBC - National Significance: Official Pre-Event to India AI Impact Summit 2026 - Opening Remarks: Dean Madan Pillutla emphasized interdisciplinary collaboration & responsible innovation

- Major Initiatives Highlighted: - Telangana Data Exchange - Telangana AI Mission - Quantum Roadmap (aligned with India’s National Quantum Mission) - Themes: Responsible AI adoption, digital trust, cybersecurity, innovation, and inclusive growth
